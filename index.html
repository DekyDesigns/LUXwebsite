The Studio

LUX operates a full-feature pre-visualization studio on the north side of Atlanta. `This is 'where the magic happens', we program all of our shows in this space. Located a few steps from the main subway station, the LUX studio is a short ride away from the busiest airport in the world, Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. This provides a convenient way to bring in designers and management from all over for collaborative projects that otherwise couldn't happen in smaller cities. Capable of handling the demands of programming projects large and small, this room is arguably the most state-of-the-art pre-visualization suite in the region. GrandMA2 and GrandMA3 (Light and Full-Size) consoles provided and set up in advance, upon request. Get in touch for more information on rentals!

Features

  • 8x displays throughout the room (3x DisplayPort touch screen, 1x 120" projector, 3x 1080p displays, 1x 4K LED TV)
  • 2x Windows PC Rack Servers (1 is a custom-built 3D server built for intense rendering/lighting visualization)
  • Sit/Stand desk capable of accommodating up to 2x GrandMA2/MA3 Full Size
  • Gigabit networking & internet
  • Professional 8" studio monitors
  • Theater-style seating with motorized recline
  • Collaborative space
  • 24-hour access
